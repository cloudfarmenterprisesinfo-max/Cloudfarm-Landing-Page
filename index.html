Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about scaling with Cloudfarm Enterprises.

Do you charge per message markups?
No, we bypass third-party Business Solution Providers (BSPs). By connecting you directly to the Meta Cloud API, you only pay Meta's raw base rate (approx. ₹0.86 per marketing message).
What is required to get started?
You will need a fresh phone number (not currently used on the standard WhatsApp app), your GST/business documents for Meta verification, and a valid credit card for Meta's billing platform.
Can I still use the WhatsApp Business app on my phone?
Yes, with Meta's recent updates, you can use our automated API solutions while simultaneously running the WhatsApp Business app on your phone for manual replies.
How does the Catalogue Development work?
If you don't have a professional catalogue, our team will design, generate PDFs, and sync your inventory directly into your WhatsApp Business profile to ensure high conversion rates.
